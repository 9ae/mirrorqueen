Antanee grew up believing loving someone is weakness.

Her father left her mother when she was pregnant with Antanee, hence then her mother continues to make bad decisions when it came to men in her life. She eventually developed addictive tendencies and got sent to rehab. Antanee was sent to live with her grandmother in Sunset Hills.

Antanee does not start out as the action girl heroine. She is cautious, rational, and doesn't go rushing into danger. At the beginning of her adventure she is often hiding, wishing Leo was here, because he would be brave enough to face it. Eventually she decides that Joren isn't here, and she begins to in still his spirit inside her.
